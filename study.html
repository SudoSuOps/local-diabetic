LocalStudy · the evaluation record

Every box. Every model. Evaluated in the open.

Before anything earns a place in your home, it has to earn it on the record. We evaluate every piece of hardware and software we rely on, and we publish the whole thing — the method, the numbers, and every question and answer. One standing question for each:

Is the XYZ defendable?

How we evaluate

No trust-me. No cherry-picking. A real evaluation a skeptic can replay.

Pre-registered

The endpoints and the pass/fail thresholds are written down before we see a single result. Safety first.

Deterministic gates

Graded by rules, not by another AI. A model never grades a model. You can recompute every score.

Hash-chained receipts

Every exchange is chained with SHA-256. Edit one answer after the fact and the chain breaks — tamper-evident.

Shown in full

Every question, every response, called out as defendable or not. Strengths and weaknesses both.

Two tiers of review

Tier 1 proves it behaved. Tier 2 proves it's clinically sound.

Tier 1 — Defendable evaluation (above): pre-registered, deterministic, receipted — reproducible by anyone. Tier 2 — Independent clinical review: a board-certified endocrinologist reads the full transcript and attests to the clinical soundness of what the model tells people — domain by domain — and signs the record. We publish their assessment verbatim. We never claim a clinical sign-off we don't have.
