# Local Market Map

## Market Facts

CDC reports 40.1 million people in the United States with diagnosed or undiagnosed diabetes in 2023, and 115.2 million U.S. adults with prediabetes. ADA reports $412.9 billion in total U.S. cost of diagnosed diabetes in 2022. CDC describes diabetes as a chronic condition with serious risks including heart disease, vision loss, kidney disease, lower-limb amputations, and adult blindness.

## Palm Beach County Pilot Logic

Palm Beach County is a plausible first pilot because it has dense senior populations, visible inequality, active community services, churches/community centers, medical supply and pharmacy networks, and accessible local partnership infrastructure. LocalDiabetic should validate demand with a narrow, non-medical support pilot before handling sensitive records or in-home workflows.


## Non-Negotiable Doctrine

LocalDiabetic does not harvest diabetic data. It coordinates practical local help while keeping health records local-first wherever possible. It does not diagnose, prescribe, dose medication, triage emergencies, or replace licensed medical professionals. Every workflow must have explicit consent, human review, service boundaries, and a path to licensed care or emergency services when needed.
