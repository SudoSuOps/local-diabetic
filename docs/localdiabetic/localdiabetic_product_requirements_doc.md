# LocalDiabetic Product Requirements Document

## Product

LocalDiabetic.com and LocalDiabetic Node MVP.

## Users

People living with diabetes, family caregivers, donors, volunteers, vendors, clinicians, community partners, and local node operators.

## MVP Requirements

- Landing page with clear positioning.
- Get Help flow with emergency warning.
- Care Packs page and request/sponsor CTA.
- Vendor directory/intake.
- Volunteer intake.
- Donate/sponsor page.
- For Families page.
- For Clinics page.
- LocalDiabetic Vault page.
- Privacy Doctrine page.
- Contact page.
- Static docs: safety policy, vendor checklist, care pack matrix.

## Functional Requirements

- Collect only minimum necessary non-emergency request data.
- Send request receipts.
- Categorize requests.
- Track care pack fulfillment.
- Track vendor verification state.
- Track volunteer onboarding state.
- Log incidents.
- Export monthly impact metrics.

## Nonfunctional Requirements

- Static-first.
- No trackers by default.
- No patient record upload in MVP.
- Accessible mobile-first UI.
- Plain-language safety boundaries.
- Security headers.
- Audit trail for requests and partner vetting.

## Out of Scope

- Diagnosis.
- Treatment recommendations.
- Medication dosing.
- Emergency triage.
- Wound-care protocols.
- Prescription fulfillment.
- Unreviewed AI decisions.
- Health data resale or ad targeting.


## Non-Negotiable Doctrine

LocalDiabetic does not harvest diabetic data. It coordinates practical local help while keeping health records local-first wherever possible. It does not diagnose, prescribe, dose medication, triage emergencies, or replace licensed medical professionals. Every workflow must have explicit consent, human review, service boundaries, and a path to licensed care or emergency services when needed.
