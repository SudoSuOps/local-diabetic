# Local Care Pack Strategy

Care packs translate LocalDiabetic from concept into visible help. They must avoid prescription items, avoid wound-treatment protocols, and avoid medical claims. Packs should provide organization, reminders, storage, comfort, checklists, and resource navigation.

## Fulfillment Model

1. Request received with non-emergency disclaimer.
2. Human reviews eligibility and urgency.
3. If medical issue appears urgent, respond with emergency/clinician escalation language.
4. Pack assembled from approved SKU categories.
5. Delivery/pickup logged.
6. Follow-up asks only operational outcome: received, useful, missing items, safety incident.


## Non-Negotiable Doctrine

LocalDiabetic does not harvest diabetic data. It coordinates practical local help while keeping health records local-first wherever possible. It does not diagnose, prescribe, dose medication, triage emergencies, or replace licensed medical professionals. Every workflow must have explicit consent, human review, service boundaries, and a path to licensed care or emergency services when needed.
