🐝 About

I'm Donovan. I'm a Type 1 diabetic.

Not "founder." Not a title. Just a person who's been living this for a long time — and who got tired of carrying it alone.

I've lived the full continuum. I've lost toes. I've had more foot surgeries than I can count on both hands. I've spent days in hospital rooms, come home to a kitchen table covered in discharge papers and new medications and supply boxes, and tried to make sense of all of it while just trying to feel okay.

If you're diabetic, or you love someone who is, you already know the shape of it:

  • wake up and check
  • carry supplies everywhere
  • fight with insurance
  • sit in waiting rooms
  • worry about your feet
  • have a bad day — and start over tomorrow

I know it because I do it.

Why LocalDiabetic exists

I didn't build LocalDiabetic from a market study. I built it from my own scars — the thing I needed, and the thing I think you need too.

A trusted local helper. A place for your records, your reminders, the daily wins, and real support — built around privacy, usefulness, and everyday life. Your information stays on a box you own. It never leaves your house. We just help you carry the day.

And it costs you nothing. The compute pays for it, so you never have to. Give people what they need, not what you have.

A diabetic, not a company

A founder has customers. A diabetic has fellow travelers. I'd rather be the second one.

I'm not building a company that talks at diabetics. I'm building a place where a diabetic is talking with other diabetics — sharing what I've learned the hard way, so the next person doesn't have to learn it that hard.

I'm the first member of this community, not the boss of it. We're figuring it out together, one day at a time.

The DailyLocal is where I write it down — one small win at a time. Foot care, your numbers, real days. No noise.
