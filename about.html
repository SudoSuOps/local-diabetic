🐝 About

I'm Donovan. I'm a Type 1 diabetic — and I call it a gift.

Not "founder." Not a title. Just a guy who lives this every single day — and who decided a long time ago to win at it.

I haven't lost to diabetes. I've won — and I've won big. It lives with me 24/7: every meal, every check, every morning. I stopped treating that as a burden a long time ago and started seeing it for what it really is — a gift that taught me discipline, gratitude, and how to truly take care of myself and the people I love. I celebrate it.

Yes, I've been through the hard parts — surgeries, hospital stays, long recoveries. I came through every one of them standing. That's not losing. That's a man winning, one day at a time.

If you're diabetic, or you love someone who is, you know it's a 24/7 life:

  • wake up and check
  • carry supplies everywhere
  • handle the insurance
  • show up at the appointments
  • take care of your feet
  • have a hard day — and win the next one

I know it because I live it — and I wouldn't trade the person it's made me.

The rest of me

Diabetes is part of my story — and a part I'm grateful for. But it isn't the whole story.

I'm a father of four. I've been married to my wife for thirty years. And I've spent three decades in commercial real estate — a top 1% broker in the country at what I do.

I don't say that to brag. I say it so you know one thing: this disease didn't shrink my life — it sharpened it. I've closed big deals and I've recovered in hospital beds. I've raised four kids and I've managed this every single day. All of it at once — and all of it, winning.

That's the whole picture — and it's why I can look you in the eye and mean it: you can win at this life, and win big. And you don't have to do it alone.

Why LocalDiabetic exists

I built LocalDiabetic from my own life — the thing I needed, and the thing I want for you. Not just to get by with diabetes. To win at it. To celebrate the life you've got.

A trusted local helper. A place for your records, your reminders, your daily wins, and real support — built around privacy, usefulness, and everyday life. Your information stays on a box you own. It never leaves your house. We just help you carry the day — and win it.

And it costs you nothing. The compute pays for it, so you never have to. Give people what they need, not what you have.

A diabetic, not a company

A founder has customers. A diabetic has fellow travelers. I'd rather be the second one.

I'm not building a company that talks at diabetics. I'm building a place where a diabetic is talking with other diabetics — sharing what I've learned winning at this, so the next person gets there faster.

I'm the first member of this community, not the boss of it. We're winning at this together, one day at a time.

The DailyLocal is where I write it down — one small win at a time. Foot care, your numbers, real days. No noise.
