Diabetic foot care starts with one small daily habit:

**Look at your feet.**

Top, bottom, toes, heels — every day.

Cuts, redness, swelling, blisters, dry cracks, sore spots, color changes.

Small things matter because small things can become big things fast.

- Warm water.
- Dry well.
- Clean socks.
- Good shoes.
- No barefoot walking.

LocalDiabetic is being built around simple daily wins like this — real-life reminders, trusted guidance, and local support for diabetics and caregivers.

The good stuff, no noise.

🐝 localdiabetic.com

---

*General education, not medical advice — if you see a wound, sore, or color change that worries you, call your care team. With neuropathy you may not feel an injury, so the daily look is what catches it. For an emergency, call 911.*
