Your diabetes info should not be scattered everywhere.

One lab result in an email. One prescription in a pharmacy app. One doctor note in a portal. One supply receipt in a text. One question you meant to ask… gone.

That's the problem. Diabetes creates a lot of little pieces — and when life gets busy, those pieces get lost.

The win is simple: **a private diabetes vault.**

- Your A1C history.
- Your medication list.
- Your foot-care notes.
- Your insurance cards.
- Your supply orders.
- Your doctor questions.
- Your emergency contacts.

All in one place. Close to you. Owned by you. Ready when you need it.

That's what LocalDiabetic is building toward. Not more noise — a better place for the important stuff.

The good stuff, no noise.

🐝 localdiabetic.com

General education, not medical advice.
