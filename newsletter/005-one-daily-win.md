Some days diabetes feels like a full-time job.

Numbers. Meals. Meds. Appointments. Supplies. Feet. Sleep. Stress.

It's a lot.

So here's the win for today:

**Pick one thing you can make easier tomorrow.**

- Refill the meter strips.
- Put socks by your shoes.
- Write down your last A1C.
- Prep the snack.
- Set the reminder.
- Ask the question you keep forgetting.

Diabetes is daily — so the wins can be daily too.

Small. Simple. Repeatable.
