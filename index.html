What it does for you

Reminders that find you

Meds, foot checks, appointments, supplies — gentle nudges to your phone and watch, 24/7, so nothing important slips.

Your records, safe at home

The two-minute habit that saves toes — a guided check, a photo timeline, and a nudge every day. Feet come first.
