My A1C came back 6.9%. Is that okay?
A 6.9% A1C is an estimate of your average glucose over about three months — roughly 151 mg/dL. For many adults with diabetes that’s near a common target, and yours is down from 7.4%, which is real progress. The trend matters more than any single number.
